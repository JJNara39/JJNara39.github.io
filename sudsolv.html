Sudoku Solver

I browsed several Sudoku solving methods and settled on this one.
It includes a GUI to make it easy to communicate the puzzle effectively. It uses a backtracking algorithm, so it solves a square but backtracks to others to make sure they work. Unfortunately, at the moment, I am unable to code it in Javascript and I am not the original author of this GUI, that honor goes to techwithtim on GitHub, found here. I am making sure to understand how each step works before claiming any knowledge of it. Once I make my own version I shall upload it and link it here.
